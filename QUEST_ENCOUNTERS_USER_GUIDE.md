## FAQ

**Q: Can I retry if I fail?**
A: No, failure ends the quest. You'll need to accept a new quest.

**Q: Do I get any reward for failing?**
A: No rewards for failure. But you can try again with another quest.

**Q: Can I change my choice after clicking?**
A: No, the button is one-time use. Choose wisely!

**Q: How many quests can I do per day?**
A: As many as you complete! No daily limit, but quest timers add up.

**Q: Does my faction matter?**
A: Yes! Different factions have different quests. Choose carefully when selecting allegiance.

**Q: What happens to reputation if I fail?**
A: You gain no reputation, but your standing doesn't drop either.
