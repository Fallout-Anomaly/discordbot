# Controls & Keybinds

## Core Movement
- **Movement**: WASD
- **Sprint**: Left Shift
- **Run/Walk Toggle**: C or Caps Lock
- **Sneak**: Left Ctrl
- **Jump**: Spacebar
- **Auto-Move**: X

## Combat Controls
- **Attack/Fire**: Mouse 1
- **Aim/Block**: Mouse 2
- **Bash/Grenade**: Left Alt
- **Reload/Ready**: R
- **VATS (Bullet Time)**: Q
- **Holster Weapon**: J
