steam://controllerconfig/377160/3429632432                                                          The layout I've been using. If you use , go to mod config for uneducated shooter and set lean, left and right, to B and M. Make sure Toggle Leaning and only in ADS are on. Then set the backpack button in IAA backpacks to L. Still haven't tested power armor so you'll have to hold E manually to get out i suppose. Also feel like criticals and VAFS could be in a better spot but everything else is pretty solid. All controls are regular fallout4 controls except the following; You double tap LB for VAFS focus. You can lean, while aiming, with the left and right bumpers. Select for PipBoy and hold select for flashlight. Double tap Y to dodge, hold Y for a critical in VAFS. Press B to crouch. Hold B to rest at a campfire, for the CRACKLE mod(not tested), or double tap B for backpack inventory. Double tap X to lower weapon. Hold the down directional arrow for the quick inventory wheel menu, and tap up for favorites. Hold the left and right directional arrows for your visor and night vision(not tested). Hold the left stick, while not moving, to toggle condition boy and survival stats(Immersive Hud). Hold the start button to toggle the FOV depth blurriness. Tap the right stick to change view. You can try holding right stick to exit power armor but this isn't tested yet either. Also, I had trouble setting up supply lines but was able to do it, without any issue, by disabling the controller support mod in mo2. The controls will be a little different, but you can still play on controller like this after setting it up. It's not my preference so it's mostly untested as well. Disabling it temporarily though will allow you to set up supply lines in build mode.
