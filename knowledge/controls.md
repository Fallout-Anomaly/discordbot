# Controls & Keybinds

## Combat & Movement
- **VAFs (Slow-Mo)**: `G` (Replacer for VATS).
- **Criticals**: `Z` (While in VAFs).
- **Leaning**: `Q` and `E` (Uneducated Shooter - note: usually needs to be enabled in MO2/MCM *after* leaving the vault).
- **Grenades/Explosives**: Hold `Left Alt` (The bash key).
- **Bash**: Tap `Left Alt`.
- **Jump**: `Spacebar`. (If it doesn't work on startup, ensure no controllers are plugged in or check keybinds in the ESC menu).

## Utilities & UI
- **Radial Menu**: `H`. Used for quick access to meds, cigarettes, etc.
- **Wipe Gas Mask**: `Shift + K`. Use this to clear blood or rain from your visor.
- **Night Vision (NVG)**: `Shift + U`.
- **Toggle HUD/Widgets**: `F2` (Immersive HUD).
- **MODExplorer**: `\` (Backslash).
- **Armor Plates**: `Y` (Replace armor plate).
- **ENB Menu**: `End` or `Delete`.
- **Dodge**: `N` (Enhanced Movement).
- **Workshop**: `Home`.

## MCM Settings
- Most mod-specific controls can be remapped in the **MCM (Mod Configuration Menu)** under the respective mod names (e.g., VAFs, Enhanced Movement, Gas Masks of the Wasteland).
